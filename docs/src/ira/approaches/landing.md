# Landing from an Approach

This page covers *Task E. Landing from an Instrument Approach* from the [FAA-S-ACS-8C Instrument Rating Airplane Airman Certification Standards](https://www.faa.gov/training_testing/testing/acs/instrument_rating_airplane_acs_8.pdf).

## Objective

To determine the applicant exhibits satisfactory knowledge, risk management, and skills associated with performing procedures for a landing from an instrument approach.
