# Turn-and-Slip Indicator / Turn Coordinator

* **Turn coordinator**
  * Roll rate (initially)
  * Rate of turn (after it stabilizes)
  * Quality of turn
  * Contains **inclinometer** - the ball - "step on the ball" to coordinate flight
* **Turn and slip indicator**
  * Rate of turn
  * Quality of turn
